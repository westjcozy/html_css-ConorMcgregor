Conor Mcgregor

Irish mixed martial artist known for his charisma and knockout power

Conor McGregor is an Irish mixed martial artist known for his striking skills and charismatic personality.
He is a former UFC featherweight and lightweight champion, making history as the first fighter to hold two titles simultaneously. McGregor is one of the biggest stars in MMA, both for his achievements in the octagon and his larger-than-life persona.
